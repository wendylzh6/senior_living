🔴 Structural Pain Points
  • 📞
    Missed inquiries. Families call at night, on weekends — understaffed teams miss calls. A missed inquiry often means a missed move-in worth $50K+/year.
  • 🐢
    Slow lead response. Industry average response time is 4–24 hours. Studies show response within 5 minutes increases conversion by 9×.
  • 👥
    Staff turnover. Sales counselors leave, taking relationship context with them. Leads go cold. There's no institutional memory.
  • 📋
    Manual workflows. Tour scheduling, follow-up emails, maintenance requests, FAQ responses — all still done by hand at most communities.
  • 💔
    Long, emotional journey. Families research for 2+ years across dozens of touchpoints. Staying present throughout that journey is nearly impossible without automation.
  • 💰
    Over-reliance on aggregators. Many operators pay A Place for Mom, Caring.com, etc. — high CPL, low quality. Owned channels are underdeveloped.
🟢 Growth Tailwinds
  • 📈
    Baby Boomer demand surge. Peak Boomer aging hits 2025–2035. Demand for assisted living and memory care is accelerating rapidly.
  • 🏠
    Independent living recovery. Post-pandemic occupancy is rebounding — independent living saw double-digit inquiry growth in 2025.
  • 🔍
    AI reshaping search. Aggregator dominance is cracking. Web form submissions to community websites are rising as AI search surfaces direct listings.
  • 🌐
    Tech openness rising. 70% of senior living orgs now use AI for predictive analytics. Operators are finally receptive to software.
  • 🏗️
    Capital reopening. After tight years, construction and acquisition activity in senior living is picking back up in 2026.
  • 🤝
    Family-driven digital channels. Adult children — the real buyers — live online. SMS, email, and chat are their native channels.
🤖 Where AI Can Step In — Meaningfully
Instant Lead Engagement
Respond to every inquiry in under 90 seconds — at 2am, on Sunday, during a holiday. Families searching in a crisis don't wait. First responder wins.
Nurture Over the Long Journey
Automated, personalized follow-up over weeks and months — so when the family is finally ready to decide, Uniti's client is top of mind.
Win-Back Campaigns
Re-engage families who went quiet 3, 6, 12 months ago. Life circumstances change. The agent can restart conversations with empathy and new info.
Resident & Staff Support
Maintenance requests, balance checks, FAQs, gate access — resolved instantly without pulling a care staff member away from a resident.
Tour Scheduling Automation
Qualify prospects and book tours automatically — synced to counselor calendars, no back-and-forth email chains.
Omnichannel Presence
Phone, SMS, email, WhatsApp, and chat — families communicate across all of these. One agent, all channels, consistent voice.

Key consideration: Families aren't ready for a sales pitch. They need information and they need it now.

Google, A Place for Mom, Reddit, reviews. Obsessive, solo, nocturnal. A shortlist of 3–5 communities gets built. Strong websites and fast chat responses win inclusion.
Key consideration: Cost, location, care level, and reputation are evaluated simultaneously.

03

Family contacts 3–5 communities at once. Responding within 5 minutes increases conversion 9×. First to respond earns disproportionate trust — even if the care is identical.
Key consideration: If the answer takes 24 hours, the family has mentally moved on.
🤖 Uniti: <90 sec response on every channel. Answers availability, care levels, cost. Books the tour before competitors reply.
04
Tours & Site Visits 👧 Adult child  |  👴 Senior  |  👨‍👩‍👧 Siblings (fly in)
2–3 communities toured. The senior's gut reaction is decisive. Tours get rescheduled, no-showed, or ghosted without proper follow-up.
Key consideration: Does it feel like a home or a hospital? Staff warmth matters as much as amenities.
🤖 Uniti: Auto-scheduling synced to counselor calendars. SMS reminders. Post-tour follow-up within the hour.
05
Family Deliberation 👧 Lead child  |  👨‍👩‍👧 All siblings  |  💼 Advisors  |  👨‍⚕️ Physician
The lead sibling gets buy-in from everyone. Long-distance siblings push back. Finances get scrutinised. The senior resists. This phase stalls for weeks — communities that stay present win. Most go quiet.
Key consideration: Consensus is fragile. One unanswered call can reopen the whole decision.
🤖 Uniti: Warm, timed nurture sequences. Win-back campaigns for families who've gone silent. Stay top of mind without pressure.
06
Decision & Move-In 👧 Adult child  |  👴 Senior  |  Sales counselor
Usually triggered by urgency or emotional readiness. Moves fast once committed. Logistics — downsizing, care assessments, paperwork — all need smooth coordination.
Key consideration: Friction kills late-stage deals. Slow responses cause last-minute cold feet.
🤖 Uniti: Full conversation history handed off to counselor. Move-in checklist and comms delivered automatically via SMS.
✓
Post Move-In: Ongoing 👴 Resident  |  👧 Family  |  🧑‍⚕️ Caregivers
Worth $50K–$120K/year per resident. Families check in constantly. Residents have maintenance needs, billing questions, activity requests. Staff can't be the 24/7 contact for all of it.
Key consideration: Responsiveness post move-in determines reviews, referrals, and retention.
